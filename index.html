How Super works with Meta Display glasses

1. Hands‑free intent

You speak or tap a lightweight prompt on your Meta Display. This is where Siri, ChatGPT, Gemini, or Grok typically stop — answering questions.

2. Super becomes the action layer

Super receives the intent and operates a real browser or desktop session, clicking, typing, and navigating exactly like a human would.

3. Cache the hard parts

Login flows, navigation paths, and repetitive steps are saved into a durable computer-use cache, reducing friction and cost on future runs.

4. Confirm and complete

For sensitive actions, Super pauses for confirmation. Wearables stay assistive — not autonomous — by design.
